About Forex Trading

Forex trading, also known as foreign exchange trading, is the act of buying and selling currencies to make a profit. It’s the largest and most liquid financial market globally, with over $6 trillion traded daily. Forex operates 24/5, providing flexibility for traders worldwide.

Unlike other financial markets, Forex offers leverage, enabling traders to control larger positions with less capital. However, this comes with risks that can be mitigated with proper knowledge and strategies. Our course equips you with these tools, teaching you how to analyze the market, manage risks, and create a profitable trading system.
